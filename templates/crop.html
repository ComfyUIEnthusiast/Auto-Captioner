✂️ Crop & Process Video

1. Select crop area: Click and drag on the video to select the crop region. Use resize handles (corners & edges) to adjust.

2. Set In/Out markers: Play the video and press I to set In point, O to set Out point. Only the selected segment will be processed.

3. Choose resolution: Select a preset or enter custom output resolution.

Navigate: Click the timeline, or use keyboard: ← → seek ±5s, Space play/pause, j/k seek ±3s slowly, ,/. seek ±1 frame.
